This plugin uses Google API to show **Google Business Profile Reviews** on your WordPress website and leave a powerful impact on your customer's decision-making process.

You can show off the live google reviews your customers have given you on your website and collect your google business reviews directly from your website.

Showing Google Business Reviews on your helps you grab your website visitors attention and gives an instant credibility for your busiess, which helps you sell more and boost your SEO efforts. 

The <a href="https://wp-hut.com/google-business-reviews-plugin-for-wordpress/">Premium Version</a> of the plugin gets you better results with detailed reviews, website notifications and many more features!

== Plugin Features ==

* Auto updates on Google reviews and ratings
* Beautiful and responsive floating widget to show Google reviews
* **Collect new Google Reviews** directly and increase your Google Rating with a **'Write a Review'** button
* No Shortcodes, No Widgets, No complicated setup.
* In build support system from the dashboard
